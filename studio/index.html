What Studio does

You bring a design problem. We solve it. Then we build it.

Studio is not a commission in the art sense. It's engineering + design + fabrication. You describe what you need — a sculptural ceiling, a hurricane-rated fin facade, a carved wall that wraps around a curved staircase — and we figure out how to make it real in Corian.

We've solved problems like this before. The projects below prove it.
