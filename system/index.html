How it works

From raw Corian to installed wall

Every panel is CNC-carved to exact dimensions with interlocking edges that snap together on-site. The result: a seamless surface with invisible joints.

01

DuPont™ Corian® solid surface — non-porous, homogeneous, repairable

02

CNC Carving

3-axis CNC routers cut the design and the patented interlocking edge profile

03

Finished Panel

Each panel leaves the shop with its design carved and edges precision-cut

04

Installed Wall

Panels interlock on-site — less than 1/32″ seam. No grout. No field cutting.
